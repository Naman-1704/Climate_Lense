The Project

About ClimateLens

A platform that bridges the gap between current events and climate understanding — connecting news to its ecological consequences, ethical weight, and pathways for action.

The Problem

Climate change is one of the most critical global challenges of our time. Yet most news platforms focus only on reporting events — not their environmental consequences, ethical responsibilities, or long-term societal impact. People consume information without truly understanding its implications. ClimateLens was built to fill this gap.

🌡️

Environmental Analysis
